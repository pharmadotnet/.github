# pharma net

* **[page](https://pharmanet.bilsul.com)** : linking pharmacies and medicine storage.
* **[dealer-docs](https://dealer.docs.pharmanet.bilsul.com)** : a person or business that buys products (often in bulk) to resell them to consumers or other businesses
* **[seller-docs](https://seller.docs.pharmanet.bilsul.com)** : any person or entity that transfers ownership of goods or services in exchange for money. A seller can be a manufacturer, a distributor, a retailer, or a private individual.
